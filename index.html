Frequently Asked Questions

Can I access Richer courses on mobile devices? −

Yes, absolutely! Richer is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android, allowing you to watch lectures, complete assignments, and track your progress on the go.

What types of courses are available on Richer? +

We have a diverse catalog of courses covering multiple domains including academic subjects, professional development, digital marketing, programming, web design, photography, video editing, and much more. Whether you're looking to upskill or learn something entirely new, we have courses for everyone.

How do I sign up for Richer? +

Signing up is quick and easy! Simply visit our website, click on the "Sign Up" button, and provide your name, email address, and a secure password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for even faster registration.

What is Richer? +

Richer is an innovative e-learning platform designed to provide accessible, affordable, and practical online education to learners worldwide. We offer a wide range of courses taught by industry experts, enabling students to learn at their own pace and earn recognized certifications.

Richer is an innovative e-learning platform for personal branding, digital marketing, and creating passive income streams.
