Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Richer ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit our website at www.Richer.com, including all related media, mobile applications, and social media platforms.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide: Name, email address, phone number, and account credentials when you register on our platform.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing address (processed securely through payment gateways).
  • Learning Information: Course progress, assignments, grades, and quiz results.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our services
  • Processing transactions and sending related information
  • Sending promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer support
  • Monitoring and analyzing platform usage patterns

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Secure password storage using encryption algorithms
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors for transaction processing
  • Analytics providers to understand usage patterns
  • Law enforcement when required by law

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. We use cookies for analytics, personalization, and remembering your preferences.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (in accordance with applicable laws)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@Richer.com
Address: New Delhi, India

Disclaimer: This privacy policy is subject to change at any time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our website.
