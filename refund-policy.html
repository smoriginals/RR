Refund Policy

Full transparency on our refund guarantee

Last Updated: December 2024

Our Commitment

At Richer, we're confident in the quality of all our courses and materials. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free refund guarantee.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request refund within 30 days of purchase
  • You have not accessed more than 30% of the course content
  • You have a valid reason for dissatisfaction
  • The course has not been completed or certified

Refund Timeframe

Standard Courses: 30-day refund window from purchase date

Certification Programs: 14-day refund window from purchase date

Bulk Purchases: 7-day refund window from purchase date

How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Visit your account dashboard and navigate to "My Courses"

Step 2: Select the course you wish to refund and click "Request Refund"

Step 3: Provide reason for refund in the form and submit

Step 4: Our team will review within 2-3 business days

Refund Processing

Once your refund is approved:

  • Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal refunds: 2-5 business days
  • Bank transfers: 7-15 business days

Non-Refundable Items

The following purchases cannot be refunded:

  • Courses purchased more than 30 days ago
  • Courses where the user has accessed more than 30% of content
  • Completed and certified courses
  • Downloadable resources or eBooks purchased separately
  • Gift cards or promotional credits

Course Access After Refund

Upon refund approval, your access to the course will be revoked within 24 hours. You will no longer have access to course materials, certificates, or assignments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a refund for a subscription plan?

Yes, subscriptions can be cancelled anytime. You'll retain access until your current billing period ends, after which no further charges will apply.

What if I lost my certificate?

If you've completed a course, you cannot request a refund. However, you can request a certificate replacement at no additional cost by contacting support.

Can I transfer my course to someone else instead of refunding?

Yes! You can transfer course access to another email address within 7 days. Contact our support team to arrange the transfer.

Will I lose my course progress if I request a refund?

Yes, all course progress, certificates, and completion records will be removed upon refund approval.
