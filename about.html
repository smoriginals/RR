About Richer

Learn more about our company and our mission to transform education

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Richer began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location. What started as a small initiative by our founder Rajesh Kumar has grown into a thriving community of over 300,000 active learners.

We recognized that traditional education had limitations - it was expensive, geographically restricted, and often didn't align with real-world industry needs. Our platform was built to bridge this gap, providing affordable, relevant, and practical online courses taught by industry experts.

Today, Richer is recognized as one of India's leading e-learning platforms, with courses spanning digital marketing, programming, design, and personal development.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To democratize quality education and empower individuals to achieve their goals through accessible, affordable, and practical online learning. We aim to eliminate barriers to education and create opportunities for lifelong learning for millions of students worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading online learning platform, transforming lives and building a vibrant community of accomplished lifelong learners. We envision a future where quality education is universally accessible and everyone has the opportunity to pursue their dreams.

Our Values

We believe in accessibility, quality, innovation, and community. Everything we do is guided by these core values, ensuring we deliver exceptional learning experiences while maintaining integrity and commitment to our students' success.

Our Journey

2018
Founded Richer

Our journey began with a vision to democratize education. We launched our first course on digital marketing to a small group of enthusiastic learners.

2019
Reached 10K Students

Our platform gained traction with students from across India. We expanded our course catalog to include programming and design courses.

2020
Global Expansion

With the rise of online learning, we expanded internationally and reached students in 50+ countries. We launched our mobile apps for iOS and Android.

2021
100K+ Active Students

Surpassed 100,000 active learners. We introduced interactive live sessions and one-on-one mentorship programs to enhance learning outcomes.

2022
500+ Courses Launched

Our course library expanded to over 500 courses across multiple domains. We partnered with industry leaders to create the most relevant courses.

2023-2024
300K+ Community

Became India's #1 e-learning platform with 300,000+ active students. Won multiple awards for innovation and educational excellence.
